Social Media Management

Posting

The act of sharing content on digital platforms such as social media and websites. It aims to share information, engage with the audience, and promote products or services. Effective posting relies on timing, consistency, and content relevance to the audience’s interests.

Content Creation

The process of producing original and valuable content for digital platforms to attract the target audience. It includes articles, videos, images, and podcasts, aiming to provide useful or entertaining information to enhance brand visibility and support marketing goals.

Post Scheduled

Refers to content that has been pre-planned and set to be published at a specific time or date in the future. This feature is commonly used in social media management tools, allowing businesses or individuals to prepare and schedule posts ahead of time, ensuring consistency and timely delivery of content. It helps save time, maintain an organized content strategy, and reach the audience at optimal times.

Reels

Short and engaging video clips shared on platforms like Instagram and Facebook, typically lasting between 15 to 90 seconds. They aim to capture attention and engage the audience using music and effects, and are commonly used for marketing and brand promotion.

Story

A temporary post that lasts for 24 hours on platforms like Instagram and Facebook, including images, videos, and text to increase interaction with followers in an informal way. It is used to share updates and promotions.

Hashtag

A word or phrase preceded by the “#” symbol used to categorize content and increase engagement on social media platforms, helping to reach a wider audience and boost visibility.
